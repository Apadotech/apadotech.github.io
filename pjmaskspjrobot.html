PJ Robot

Friend of the PJ Masks, this miniature inventor is the keeper of HQ and unlocks the heroesâ€™ new superpowers! PJ Robot is small, cute and has his own language of mechanical clicks, beeps and fizzes.

PJ Robot has the ability to hover above the ground, but additional details of its ability are currently unknown. It is presumed that PJ Robot can fly at high altitudes and speeds because of its wings.

In "The Mountain Prisoner," it's revealed that PJ Robot has at least four weapons installed in him, and can fight alongside the PJ Masks if needed.

In "The Wolfies Take HQ," it's revealed that PJ Robot can remote control HQ's rocket ship function.

In "PJ Robot Takes Control," it's revealed that PJ Robot knows magic tricks and juggling.

In "Gekko Takes Charge," it's revealed that PJ Robot can play music to wake people up.

PJ Robot is able to speak English, though, his speech often sounds intelligible. However, he expresses his emotions by changing the digital face on his head. He is very friendly and energetic.

PJ Robot is shaped slightly like an egg and is gray in color with a red trim design around its body and wings. It has a clear dome for a head with an aqua-colored digital face and a blue trim lightning bolt design on each side. Painted on the middle of its body is a green lizard's tail, a blue paw print, and red wings.
