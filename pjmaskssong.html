About PJ Masks

During the day, Amaya, Connor and Greg lead totally normal lives, but they transform into superheroes during the night and use their powers to fight villains and solve mysteries.

Heroes: PJ Robot, Owlette, Gekko, Catboy, Newton Star, Armadylan, Kevin, Teeny Weeny, An Yu

Villain: Night Ninja, Ninjalinos, Remeo, Robot, Robette, Luna Girl, Moth, Rip, Howler, Kevin, Munkigu, Octobella, Pharaoh Boy
